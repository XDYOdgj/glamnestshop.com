Powerful Bonding for Every Surface

Whether you're a professional or a DIY enthusiast, the PC Products PC-Clear Epoxy Adhesive Liquid 75005 is designed to provide strong, long-lasting bonding for a wide range of materials. Our clear epoxy liquid undergoes a special curing process, where it turns opaque before setting into a strong, durable bond. This feature is perfect for applications where visibility or aesthetics are important, such as hardware repairs and creative crafts.
