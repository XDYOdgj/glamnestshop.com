West System Epoxy Resin Bundle

The ultimate DIY solution with everything you need to complete your projects with confidence and precision.

High-Performance Epoxy Adhesive

Our West System epoxy resin bundle features a premium 105-B resin, a 206-B slow curing hardener, and a complete set of 300 micro pumps. The slow curing hardener allows for extended curing times, making it ideal for use in high-temperature environments.

The pale yellow resin creates a strong, durable plastic that bonds effectively with wood, fiberglass, metal, and other materials. Whether you're working on boat repairs, kayak construction, or surface coating, this epoxy system ensures reliable results with superior adhesion and strength.

Easy Mixing and Application

Designed for both professionals and DIY enthusiasts, this bundle simplifies the mixing process with calibrated micro pumps. These precision tools dispense the resin and hardener in an exact 5:1 ratio, ensuring consistent coverage and optimal bonding performance.

With no VOC emissions, this epoxy is safe for use in indoor environments and offers a clean, healthy workspace. It's perfect for a variety of projects, from restoring old furniture to building custom models, and is a must-have for anyone looking for high-quality results without the hassle.
