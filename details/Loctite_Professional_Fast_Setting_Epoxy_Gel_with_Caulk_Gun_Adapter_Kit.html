Professional-Grade Epoxy Gel for Durable Bonds

Loctite's fast-setting epoxy gel is engineered to deliver a strong, long-lasting bond for a variety of materials. Whether you're working with metal, plastic, glass, or wood, this thixotropic formula ensures perfect adhesion even in tight spaces. Its industrial-grade construction makes it ideal for professional applications where reliability is key.

Each kit comes with 1 cartridge, 1 mixer, and 1 caulk gun adapter, giving you the flexibility to apply with a standard caulking gun. This makes the process efficient and precise, perfect for both DIYers and professionals. With a 50ml volume, it's a practical choice for tackling small gaps and creases without compromising on quality.
