Epoxy Gel for Strong, Quick Bonds

Our fast-setting epoxy gel delivers a crystal clear, durable bond for a wide range of materials including plastic, metal, glass, wood, ceramic, rubber, and masonry. It's designed for use in industrial and professional environments, offering reliable performance and a toughened finish that withstands daily wear and tear.

With a 3-5 minute work time, this epoxy gel is ideal for quick bonding projects. Its medium-viscosity formula makes application smooth and precise, while ensuring a strong, long-lasting hold. Whether you're repairing household items or working on demanding construction tasks, this product is built to meet your needs.

The thixotropic properties allow it to maintain its shape and prevent sagging, making it perfect for vertical and horizontal surfaces. Its clear bondline ensures a professional appearance without visible residue, making it a great choice for both DIY enthusiasts and commercial applications.
