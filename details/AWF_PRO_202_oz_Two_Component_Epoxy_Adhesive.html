AWF PRO Two-Component Epoxy Adhesive

A powerful, fast-curing adhesive designed for strength and performance in both industrial and home environments.

AWF PRO Two-Component Rapid-Cure Epoxy Adhesive is engineered to provide exceptional bonding strength and precision for a wide range of applications. Its rapid 9-minute gel time allows for quick setup, making it ideal for time-sensitive tasks where efficiency is key. Once fully cured, this adhesive delivers an impressive compression strength of 12,600 psi, ensuring that your anchors and steel components remain secure under pressure and in harsh conditions.

One of the standout features of this product is its minimal shrinkage, which prevents gaps and maintains the integrity of your bond. Combined with its chemical resistance, it offers long-lasting durability even in extreme environments. Whether you're reinforcing concrete, attaching metal parts, or securing threaded rods, AWF PRO's epoxy adhesive is built to deliver consistent, reliable results.
