Powers Pure110+ Epoxy Adhesive - A Reliable Solution for Concrete Bonding

Our Powers Pure110+ Epoxy Adhesive is built for strength, versatility, and durability. This high-performance two-component epoxy system is designed to bond steel reinforcing bars and threaded rod to hardened concrete with exceptional reliability. Whether you're working on a new construction site or tackling repair projects, this adhesive delivers a robust bond that stands up to the toughest conditions.

With its fast-setting formula, this adhesive is perfect for quick installations in varying weather and temperature conditions. It maintains its integrity in mixed moisture environments, making it an ideal choice for foundation repair, pool walls, steps, and stairs. The Pure110+ system is trusted by professionals for its consistent performance and long-lasting results.

Key Features

  • Rated for high-strength applications with a rating of 110+
  • Two-component fast-setting adhesive for efficient bonding
  • Excellent for bonding steel reinforcing bars to concrete
  • Works in mixed moisture and temperature conditions
  • Creates a strong, durable bond ideal for construction and repair
