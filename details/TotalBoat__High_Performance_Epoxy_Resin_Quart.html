Professional-Grade Epoxy Resin System

Perfect for quick curing boat finishes or general adhesion projects. This high-performance epoxy resin system offers fast curing times and a strong, durable finish, ideal for use on wood, fiberglass, aluminum, and steel surfaces.

Key Features

  • Two-part high-performance epoxy resin system
  • Non-blushing finish for a smooth, professional appearance
  • Low odor and low VOCs, making it environmentally friendly and safe for indoor use
  • Designed for easy mixing with a 2:1 resin to hardener ratio
  • Compatible with TotalBoat 2:1 pumps (sold separately)

Why Choose This Product?

This epoxy resin is engineered for quick curing and superior results in boat building and repair.
